Hoshinoya Kyoto

This elegant and quiet hotel located on the Katsura River is surrounded by trees and can only be accessed by boat.

60 Arashiyama Nakaoshitacho, Nishikyo Ward, Kyoto, 616-0004, Jepun


Hyatt Regency Hakone

Located in the central region of Honshu inside Fuji Hakone Izu National Park, the Hyatt Regency Hakone Resort and Spa provides famous views of Mount Fuji along with convenient access to the renowned hot springs (onsens) and sightseeing spots.

1320 Gōra, Hakone, Ashigarashimo District, Kanagawa 250-0408, Jepun


Iwaso, Miyajima, Hiroshima

Offering traditional elegance in a Japanese inn that includes a traditional wooden building and cottages that date back to 1900, plus a modern annex, Iwaso is the oldest and most famous lodging on Miyajima Island.

南町-345-1 Miyajimachō, Hatsukaichi, Hiroshima 739-0522, Jepun


Ryokan Ekoin

Eko-in is a 1,000-year-old Buddhist temple that offers monastic lodging for monks, pilgrims, and tourists alike. Despite being the best-equipped ryokan on Koya-san, accommodation here is still very simple.

648-0211 Wakayama, Koyasan, Koyasan 497, Jepun


Henn na Hotel, Tokyo Bay

For the ultimate futuristic Japanese hotel experience, you need to head to Henn na Hotel, which is part-run by robots. Robotic multilingual dinosaurs man the check-in desk.

5-chōme-3-20 Fujimi, Urayasu, Chiba 279-0043, Jepun


Nine Hours Hotels, Nationwide

Staying in a capsule hotel is a unique Japanese experience, and the Nine Hours brand is an ideal option, ticking all the boxes of futuristic, modern design and minimalist functionality.

Jepun, 〒101-0054 Tokyo, Chiyoda City, Kanda Nishikichō, 3-chōme−１１−15


Keio Plaza Hotel, Tokyo

At first glance, the Keio Plaza Hotel doesn’t look like it should be on this list, but hidden beneath the classy 4-star hotel sheen is a collection of Hello Kitty-themed rooms, which feature an extravaganza of Hello Kitty paraphernalia.

2-chōme-2-1 Nishishinjuku, Shinjuku City, Tokyo 160-8330, Jepun


Hotel Public Jam, Osaka

Another classic Japan accommodation experience is staying in a love hotel – hotels that rent rooms by the hour (or night) and are dedicated to getting you in the mood, often with a variety of different themes.

5-chōme-5-12 Uehonmachi, Tennoji Ward, Osaka, 543-0001, Jepun
